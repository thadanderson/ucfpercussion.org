## How to Get Here

The Performing Arts Center (PAC) is located on UCF's main campus. The Music offices are in the south building (PAC M203), with reception on the second floor near the interior staircase. Staff are available Monday – Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM.

[Open in Google Maps →](https://goo.gl/maps/gVqeHKfnZRXzcDTH6)

## Parking

A parking pass is required on campus unless you are attending an event with designated free parking. Visit [UCF Parking Services](https://parking.ucf.edu/) for details.
